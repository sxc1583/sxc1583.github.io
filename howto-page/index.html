Easy Scrambled Eggs

For All Your Breakfast Needs

Searching for how to make this breakfast staple?

Just follow these step-by-step instructions to learn how!

Ready to Start?

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • 1 tbsp/egg of water, cream, or milk
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Butter
  • Nonstick Skillet
  • Spatula
  • Optional: Fresh Chopped Herbs, Ham, or Cheese

Beat the Eggs

Beat your eggs until they're completely blended. Add a little water, cream or milk to make them tender. Use 1 tablespoon of liquid per egg. Add a pinch of salt.

Prep the Pan

Next, heat a nonstick skillet over a medium-low flame and toss in a pat of butter. Make sure the butter coats the pan.

Add the Eggs

Then, pour in the eggs. Pause to let them heat slightly â€” gentle heat is essential.

Cook the Eggs

Move the eggs across the pan like a bulldozer so the eggs cook evenly. This takes a little time, but it's worth it.

Optional: As the eggs start to set, add chopped fresh herbs, or bits of ham or cheese.

Ready to Eat

Turn your eggs onto warmed plates and dig in!
