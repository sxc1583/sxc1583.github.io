Winter Tour: An Evening of Prokofiev and Mendelssohn

Saturday, February 15th, 2020, 8:00 p.m.

University of Miami Clarke Recital Hall

Enjoy an evening of powerful and colorful music, featuring Prokofiev's String Quartet No. 2, Arvo Part's Summa, and Mendelssohn's String Quartet No. 6. This program was performed by the quartet during their debut winter tour of South Carolina, hosted by Hilton Head's Young Artist Performance Series.

All-Stamps Concert: The Albatross and the Seal

Thursday, February 27th, 2020, 7:30 p.m.

University of Miami Gusman Concert Hall

This concert will feature all four of the Frost Stamps Ensembles working together on the newly commissioned work "The Albatross and the Seal" by Frost composition student Wesley Thompson. Each of these ensembles will also perform selections by Beethoven, Ibert, Piazzolla, Crespo, Berne, and Frisell. Also, don't miss Frost School of Music Dean Shelly Berg on piano during the newly commissioned work! It will be a great concert and we hope to see you there.
